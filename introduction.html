Definition of Customer Relationship Management

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is a system built for managing all important relationships within a company and interactions with not only existing customer but also potential customers. The objective of CRM is to nurture business relationships that can help a business to grow. A CRM system increases the connectivity of a business with its customers, enhance efficiency of business processes, and raise profitability.

The term "CRM" is normally referring to a CRM system, a perfect tool for business to be able to manage all important contacts, all relevant sales procedures, salespersons productivity, and many more business operation activities at a single platform. CRM enables the ability of a business to manage customer relationships across the entire customer lifecycle including marketing, sales, and customer service interactions.

With the capability of a CRM solution, business can focus on managing important relationships - including customers, subscribers, service users, teams, or partners. This will then allow the business to be able to find new customers, winning new business deals, and providing necessary support for loyal customers and additional services throughout the relationship.

The Usage of CRM

Sales

CRM system is helpful for managing sales in a company regardless of the company's size. CRM system comes with features where it enables a sales person to set suitable sales pipelines for different type of customers. The system also comes with important features for a sales person including reminders, chats, emails, and many more.


Business Development

With the amount of information available in the CRM system, company can make better decision in providing services or products that are suitable for the taste of their potential customers and existing customers. This also enables personalization in services and products.


Marketing

Integration with the various social media platforms helps the company in managing its marketing activities easily. More time will be spent in generating new ideas rather than wasting time in combining data gathered across social media platforms.


Recruitment

Recruiting new members can be done easily when the company website is also integrated with the system. Information regarding the newly recruited members can be collected and accessed at ease.


Customer Service

Customer service involving all social media accounts can be managed at one place. Interactions with both existing customers and potential customers helps the relevant sales person to provide the most suitable services for the customers.


Performance

CRM system helps the company's management to be able to set, track and evaluate the performance of every single sales person.
