The Benefits of CRM

Shared Information

    Connecting Business Information
  • Establish a shared platform for information processing
  • Help managing all business relationships across functions
  • Understand customers across departments
  • Employees works closely to better serve different type of relationships

Bottom Line Improvement

    Improving Bottom Line for Business
  • Sales
    1. Lead Conversion Rate
    2. Deal Size
    3. Win Rate
  • Marketing
    1. Lead Conversion Rate
    2. Qualified Leads
    3. Campaign Effectiveness
  • Also bottom line for customer service, and digital commerce

Lead Management

    Managing Leads Efficiently
  • Identify new leads
  • Add new leads
  • Categorize leads
  • Help focusing attention and energy on the right clients

Customer Lifetime Value

    Increasing Customer Lifetime Value
  • Understand customers better
  • Cross-selling opportunities
  • Upselling opportunities
  • Develop long-lasting and profitable relationships with customers

Customer Support

    Providing better customer support
  • Fast and personalised support at any time of day or night
  • High-quality services with easy access of information across departments
  • Existing orders details can be retrieved at ease
  • Record of interactions are available

Product Improvement

    Improving Products and Services
  • Real customers feedback gathered
  • Insights on customer taste
  • Understand how customer feels towards the business
  • Improve products and services accordingly
  • Spot issues as soon as possible
  • Recognize gaps with customers taste
